United Airlines

In the summer of 2019, I worked as a UX Design Intern at United Airlines in Houston, TX.

I worked under the Technical Operations team, which provides aviation maintenance, repair, and overhaul (MRO).

Within TechOps, I collaborated with designers, developers, and business architects to modernize and digitize the outdated MRO process, making it much simpler and more effective than before. Specifically, my role was to design some the interfaces for the web version of the application using Sketch. I also had the opportunity to assist in conducting usability tests and training technicians on the app at Denver International Airport.

If you would like to learn more about my work at United, feel free to contact me!
