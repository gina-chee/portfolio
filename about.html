About Me

Designer, artist, and a perpetual work-in-progress.

Hi, I'm Gina, and I am passionate about all things design, particularly user experience and designing digital products that are both beautiful and functional. My passion for creativity stems from my background in drawing and painting where I learned the fundamentals of art theory and visual communication, which I apply to my work today. Previously, I have interned at United Airlines and ViewTrade Securities Inc. To learn about the work I did there, feel free to take a look at my CV.

Currently, I am a student at NYU, obtaining a B.S. in Integrated Digital Media with a minor in studio art. It's here that I developed skills in other design fields, including web design, motion graphics, creative coding, and digital art. I am constantly working on these skills through my school and personal projects in order to improve myself, my technique, and my artistic style, which is why I consider myself a continuous WIP.

If I'm not studying or flipping through dozens of Adobe tabs, I'm probably playing with my dog, Button, and managing his social media, thrift shopping, watching true crime documentaries, or exploring the city with friends.
