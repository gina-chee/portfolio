About Me

Designer, artist, & perpetual work-in-progress.

Hey there, nice to meet you! I’m Gina, my pronouns are she/her, and I am a product designer with a love for user experience and visual design. I grew up in Sugar Land, TX (yes, Sugar Land), and currently live in Brooklyn, NY, where I am in my final year at New York University, obtaining a B.S. in Integrated Digital Media.

I’ve always had a human-centered and artistic design approach. My background in fine arts, combined with my interest in issues like accessibility, social justice, and environmental sustainability, pushes me to problem solve with inclusivity, functionality, and aesthetics in mind.

Outside of school and work, I am constantly extending my spice rack. You may also know me as a proud dog mom to Button, frequent Wikipedia reader, winged eyeliner pro, and avid thrift shopper.

Right now, I am a product designer at NYU IT. I have previously designed for Amazon, United Airlines, and ViewTrade Securities.

Interested in learning more?

Shoot me an email at ginachee@gmail.com if you want to chat about my work, museum exhibitions, or the dogs you've seen this week.
