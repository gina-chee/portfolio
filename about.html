About Me

Designer, artist, & perpetual work-in-progress.

Hey there, nice to meet you! I'm Gina, my pronouns are she/her, and I am a product designer with a love for user experience and visual design. My passion for systemic issues like accessibility, social justice, and environmental sustainability pushes me to design with functionality and inclusivity first. I am currently a senior at NYU, obtaining a B.S. in Integrated Digital Media with a minor in Studio Art.

On the other hand, I also enjoy experimenting with less functional but more thought-provoking and creative projects. You can always find me working on either a new art piece or passion project to improve my technique and artistic style, which is why I consider myself a WIP.

Alongside my academics and work, I am also a proud dog mom to Button, frequent Wikipedia reader, winged eyeliner pro, and avid thrift shopper.

Right now, I am a UX design intern at Amazon. I have previously designed for United Airlines and ViewTrade Securities Inc.

Interested in learning more?

Shoot me an email at ginachee@nyu.edu if you want to chat about my work, museum exhibitions, or the dogs you've seen this week.

Or, get to know me better through my other platforms.
