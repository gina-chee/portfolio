About Me

Designer, artist, and a perpetual work-in-progress.

Hey there, nice to meet you! I'm Gina, my pronouns are she/her, and I am passionate about all things visual design, particularly in user experience and designing digital products that are both beautiful and functional. My passion for creativity stems from my background in drawing and painting where I learned the fundamentals of art theory and visual communication, and apply that to my work today.

I am currently a third-year student at NYU, obtaining a B.S. in Integrated Digital Media with a minor in Studio Art. It's here that I developed skills in other design fields, including graphic design, web design, and motion graphics. I'm constantly working on these skills through my school and personal projects in order to improve my technique and artistic style which is why I consider myself a WIP.

If I'm not studying or flipping through dozens of Adobe tabs, I'm probably playing with my dog, Button, and managing his social media, reading Wikipedia articles, evening out my winged eyeliner, or exploring NYC's thrift stores.

This summer, I will be working for Amazon as a UX Design Intern. Previously, I have interned at United Airlines and ViewTrade Securities Inc, which you can learn more about on my CV.

Interested in learning more?

Shoot me an email at ginachee@nyu.edu if you want to chat about my work, museum exhibitions, or the dogs you've seen this week.

Or, get to know me better through my social media.
