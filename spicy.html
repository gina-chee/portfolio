Spice Rack

Welcome to the spice rack, home to all my seasoned and spicy passion projects! Aside from my professional design work, I also like to nurture my artsy and experimental side through these projects and artworks. As of now, I've mainly been interested in digital media, fine arts, and wearables, so my work on this page are categorized in that way. Some of these pieces are animated and are toggled off for accessibility. Click on the play button to get the full experience, and click again to stop the animation!

Wake Me Up At..., 2019

Laser-etched silk sleeping masks

Inspired by one of my previous works, I wanted to create these masks as both a performance piece and an assistive wearable. Flustered by capitalism and its effects on our work-life balance, I chose to make these masks after years of observing people falling asleep on subways during their commute.
