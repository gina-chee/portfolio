It may be a little meta to talk about my portfolio development on my portfolio, but my this is probably one of my favorite professional and personal projects I've ever done, so I think it's worth sharing to the rest of the world.

This project technically started during my sophomore year of college, when I came to the realization that I wanted to have a real portfolio website, instead of the Tumblr blog I was currently using to show off my work. But in reality, my journey in figuring out how I wanted to perceive myself, and how I wanted others to perceive me, has been an existential question I've been asking myself for years. This is still an incomplete quest (hence the reason for no end date), but I think every iteration of myself so far since that epiphany moment in sophomore year has been worth documenting.

To view my branding and portfolio process, you can view the case study on Behance.
