Role: UX Design Intern

Strategies: UX / UI Design

Timeline: Jun - Aug 2020

Tools used: Sketch

In the summer of 2020, I interned virtually at Amazon on the Alexa mobile design team.

At Amazon, I designed experiences for the Alexa mobile app, including feature brainstorming, wireframing, and creating my own design system. I also had the opportunity to participate in an Amazon Smart Home Hackathon where my team won a runner-up award, and design mobile interfaces for the Alexa Communications team.

Due to NDA, I am unable to share my designs publicly. However, please feel free to reach out via email or LinkedIn if you want to hear more about the work I did, or want to learn more about the overall Amazon internship experience!
